## Content Guidance

Use `content-writer` when the task involves blog posts, thought leadership, technical explainers, or product-adjacent educational content. Combine it with:
- `technical-blog-writing` for draft quality and article structure
- `attention-retention-writing` for stronger openings and better reader flow
- `blog-editorial-strategy` for audience targeting, topic framing, and editorial planning

## Commit Guidelines

- Do not include `Co-Authored-By` lines in commit messages.
- Use imperative summaries: `feat: add agent`, `fix: resolve sync issue`.
